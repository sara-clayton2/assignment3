About the Company

How We Began the Buisness

With Love Bridal Salon has grown into an award-winning bridal manufacturer from a background of 5 years as a retail store. In 1998, With Love produced their first gown. From there, With Love line was introduced and quickly gained a reputation for beautiful gowns. With Love's past experience as a retail store continues to inspire their innovative designs and outstanding customer service.

The With Love family of brands has now expanded to include the exquisite Kelvin Klien and VeraWang collection.
